Meet Prabir

Prabir Datta

Principal

Prabir Datta has over twenty five years of experience in investment advisory and risk management. Prior to founding Risk Strategy Consultants, he was Chief Operating Officer of Constellation Capital Management, a boutique multi-strategy hedge fund in New York. Before that, he was Head of Investment Management at Guidance Financial Group and Senior Vice President of the hedge fund of fund team at Lazard Asset Management. He was also Managing Director, Portfolio Management and Global Risk Management for GE Capital and Vice President in the Emerging Markets research group at JP Morgan. He holds a Ph.D. in Finance from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta and an undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani.
